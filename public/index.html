How It Works

Dressing well starts with
knowing your season.

Color season analysis identifies the specific qualities — temperature, depth, and saturation — that make colors work with your natural coloring instead of against it.

1

Identify your season

There are 12 color seasons — each defined by a unique combination of warmth, depth, and chroma. Your season is the one that mirrors your natural coloring and makes you look most alive.

2

Get your personalized palette

We'll show you exactly which colors belong to your season — from neutrals and statement colors to the specific shades to build your wardrobe around. No more guessing.

3

Shop filtered to your colors

Browse clothing pre-filtered to your exact palette. Everything you see is already in your season — so you can focus on fit, style, and what you actually love.
